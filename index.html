Naples in the spotlight

City of Naples

The city of Naples offers a wide range of historical buildings. On the way there, the tiny streets are exactly like in the movies. On top of that you'll find many places for a refreshing drink.

Views

In several places you'll have a beautiful view of the whole city. Mount Vesuvius as well as the harbour are two things you definitely must see. Enjoy and don't forget your camera!
